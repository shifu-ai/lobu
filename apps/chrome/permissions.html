Owletto permissions

Owletto only sees what you let it see. Each capability is opt-in and can be revoked at any time. Granting a permission here updates the capabilities advertised to your agent on the next poll.

Browsing history

Lets your agent see and search pages you've visited (excluding Incognito). Useful for "what was that article I read yesterday" queries.

Chrome permission: history

Bookmarks

Lets your agent see and organise your bookmarks. Useful for "save this", "find that thing I bookmarked under work" queries.

Chrome permission: bookmarks
